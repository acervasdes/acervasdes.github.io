My name is Andrea, and I’ve always had a hard time describing myself. I’m 21 years old, I’m a Mechanical Engineering major, and I’m passionate about too many things. I love biology, space, entrepreneurship, and music. I’m very enthusiastic when it comes to talking about the things I find interesting. I consider myself to be a self-learner. I would also love to create my own company, travel the world, and live on the beach.
